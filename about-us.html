Who we are?

With a legacy of over 90 years, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is proud to boast of an alumni network comprising of eminent personalities from various fields of study.

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad has been a beacon of Indian education for a number of years, since its inception in 1926 and has a rich heritage of producing excellent graduates whose works bring laurels to the institute throughout their careers. The institute acknowledges and takes immense pride in the role played by its alumni in the development and advancement of the institute. Now that the focal areas of the advancement and growth strategy of the Institute would hinge upon Internationalization, Interdisciplinary Activities, and Industrial Relations, we seek to further forge our relationship with our Alumni with a view to translate latent possibilities into effective and actual benefits for the individuals, society and nation.

The Office of International Relations and Alumni Affairs consists of a dedicated team of staff members headed by Prof. Dheeraj Kumar, Dean, International Relations and Alumni Affair and Alumni Affair and Prof. Rajeev Upadhyay, Associate Dean Alumni Affair.

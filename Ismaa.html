ISMAA

Tagline

Home / ISMAA

Over the course of the past few years, ISMAA has emerged as a key organization in executing yearlong events and activities involving our alumni members, all across the globe. It continuously endeavours to strengthen the relationship between the alumni of this institute and their alma mater. It has been instrumental in organizing Basant, the Annual Alumni Meet, publishing the Annual Yearbook and among other things like organizing regular guest lectures by distinguished alumni in the campus. The bond between the alumni and the institute is an eternal one, and ISMAA aims to help keep it that way.

ISMAA and its various chapters have been responsible for making several scholarships available to the students of the institute every year. It has also been the driving force behind developing various projects in the institute, none more prominent than the Innovation and Tinkering Lab. These help immensely in the growth and progress of the institute,

The main objectives of the association are -
1. To promote and foster a mutually beneficial interaction between the Alumni and the present students of IIT (ISM)     Dhanbad.
2. To promote and foster the spirit-de-corps amongst the alumni.
3. To encourage the Alumni to take an active interest in the progress of the Institute so as to contribute towards     the growth and development of their Alma Mater.

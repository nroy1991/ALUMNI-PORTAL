VISIT US

The Pride comes with new standard

Home / visit us

The institute welcomes members of its alumni network back to its campus for any visit, be it for the sake of business or pleasure. Visits by the alumni are really important for the students and the faculty alike, as there are no better people to assess the growth and development of the institute than its alumni.

HOW TO REACH -

RAIL

Dhanbad Junction Railway Station, located just 2.8 km from the campus, is the nearest railway station and has numerous trains connecting it with the rest of the country. Taxis are available 24/7 to ferry one to the campus, along with ride-hailing apps like Ola.

AIR

The closest commercial airports to Dhanbad are Birsa Munda Airport in Ranchi (located 140 km away) or Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport (located 269 km away). After landing at Birsa Munda Airport in Ranchi, one can proceed to take a train or a bus to Dhanbad (4-5 hours journey). If one chooses to use the airport at Kolkata instead, one can take a shuttle to Howrah Railway Station (situated 16.5 km away) and then take a train to Dhanbad, which should take around 4-5 hours.

ROAD

Dhanbad has a good road network connecting it with all major cities nearby. NH-18 and NH-19 are the major highways running through it, connecting it with cities like Ranchi, Kolkata, Jamshedpur, Bokaro, etc. There are plenty of buses plying on these roads, connecting these cities. Online portals and mobile applications like redBus can also be used to book one’s bus tickets for travel to and from Dhanbad in a hassle-free manner.
